## Mastery ontology

Use these four levels consistently across competencies (adjust wording if your HR policy uses different labels):

| Level | Typical meaning |
|-------|-----------------|
| **FOUNDATION** | Basic understanding and application **with guidance** |
| **PRACTITIONER** | Mastery of **moderately complex** problems |
| **ADVANCED** | Mastery of **complex** problems and **system-level** thinking |
| **EXPERT** | Exceptional mastery; **shapes** best practices and innovation |

## How to use

- One row per **observable behaviour** at each mastery level — avoid purely subjective traits.
- Tie examples in reviews to **specific behaviours** below.
